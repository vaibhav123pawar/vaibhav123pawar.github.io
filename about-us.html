About Us

We are a recruitment company founded in 2020 and have our head office in Pune

We are not head hunters We go a step ahead in screening and grooming the candidates as per the client requirement and then presenting them for the selection process.

Meeting the client requirement is our ultimate aim The job seekers are not only our assets, but they are also our responsibility.

Our dedicated recruitment team with a collective experience of over 20 years in the staffing industry are experts at sourcing candidates pan India We are committed to achieving excellence in the services being provided to both our clients and job seekers.
