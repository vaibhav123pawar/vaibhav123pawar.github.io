Our Process

Staff at ClafaFutura is trained in using the latest and most efficient technologies to make the recruitment process easier, smoother & seamless to find the perfect match creating a win-win situation for both, the employees and companies.

  • Connect
  • Intel Match
  • Screen & Groom
  • Handshake
  • Conclude

Connect

Client Relation Team has regular meetings with clients. To understand client requirement.

Intel Match

Recruitment team searches for the right prospect based on client requirement.

Screen & Groom

Technical team screens the prospects. HR team conditions the shortlists on soft skills.

Handshake

Recruitment team ensures that the client candidate interaction is seamless.

Conclude

HR team facilitates follow up with selects and tracks the position to closure.
